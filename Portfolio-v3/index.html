I'm Anastasija Scepak, a front end web developer/designer. Currently I'm located in the United Kingdom. I'm a passionate about front end web development/design, I like to think out of the box and use my imagination while drafting, sketching and developing/designing beautiful, yet interesting websites.

In 2013 I've graduated from Vilnius University as a translator. I've always had an interest in web development & design, but was too afraid to try to learn it. My partner was always supportive about it and he encouraged me to try a few online courses. After a finishing one of the online courses I've started building my own page.

My very first experience in making a website was when I was in my teen years. Back then I had no idea what I was doing, but I really enjoyed it. That experience in the past really helped me out while learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript & JQuery.
