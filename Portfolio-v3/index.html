Hello, I'm Anastasija Scepak a web developer/desginer, curently located in United Kingdom. I'm a passionate web designer who likes to think out of the box and use my imagination while drafting, sketching and designing beautiful and interesting websites.

I've graduated as a bachelor in philology from Vilnius University (Kaunas Faculty of Humanities) in 2013. The idea of becoming a web designer was given by my boyfriend before graduating. But at that moment my mind was set at becoming a translator. My first experience in making a website was when I was 13 years old. I made my first website dedicated to Harry Potter. To tell the truth - I didn't know what I was doing. Everything was made in HTML without using CSS or JavaScript. In a word - there was no decent design. But at that moment I was proud of myself. That experience really helped me a lot while I was and still am learning about web design and web development.
I really enjoy working on websites, polishing up my skills and learn something new.
