About Me

I'm Anastasija, but usually I introduce myself as Stacie. Originally I come from Lithuania, where I've graduated from Vilnius University as a translator. I've always had a keen interested in web development & design, but was too afraid to learn it.
At the end of 2013 I have started my journey as a Front End Web Developer/Designer.

I'm a passionate Front End Web Developer/Designer who enjoys building interesting and high quality websites.
I like to think out of the box and use imagination while drafting, sketching and designing beautiful yet simple websites.
Besides being interested in web development & design, I really enjoy photography, reading books, playing violin, travelling and exploring new places.
